Video Editor

Iowa State University, English Department | September 2019 - April 2020

The Iowa State English Department has 5 undergrad majors: English, English Education, technical communication, linguistics, and speech communication. Graduates of these majors were filmed talking about how their major helped prepare them for grad school or life after college. The videos are on the English Department Website and the LAS college YouTube channel to promote the major to perspective students.


Project

Video and Audio Editing

Nine English department alumni were filmed with several takes speaking about their experiene in the English department. I created a video for each alumni by editing the audio and video. I spent most of my time editing out the wind and construction noises in the background. I also added graphics and music and I color corrected the videos.

Audience and Purpose

The videos are intended to be hosted on the LAS YouTube Channel and English Deparment website and presented in-person at recruitment events. These videos will be especially useful now that summer orientation needs to be online due to the current COVID-19 pandemic. They are meant to recruit prospective undergraduate students who are interested in acquiring a major or minor in the English Department. These students are most likely in their last year of high school or early in their college career. The videos provide advice about having a major in the English Department, being a college student, the kinds of job students can get, and more for young students who made need to see a real person talk about how they were successful because of their LAS degree.
